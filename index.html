about us

we make things happen

PeakFestival is the largest electronic music festival held in England, taking place in the largest nation park in the UK, Peak District. It is held annually and has been designated Best Major Festival in the European Festival Awards every year since first edition in 2010. Guests come from a vast range of European countries, as well as Asia and North America, US.

Because of the actual situation, this year we have decided to keep our festival online, giving the chance to our people to enjoy their favorite festival from their own houses.
